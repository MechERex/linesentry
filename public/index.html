The Problem

One missed note.
One line stoppage.

The notes are always there. Buried in a title block, a revision triangle, a general notes section. The problem isn't the drawing. It's the process of reading it under pressure, every time, without a system.

01
Missed Drawing Requirements
Material certifications, surface treatments, testing requirements, marking specs — buried in note blocks and read manually every time. One miss and a part comes back wrong.
02
Dropped Signals
Vendor delays buried in email threads. Engineering changes posted in Teams. Production updates nobody actioned. The information exists. Nobody cross-referenced it against the job.
03
Engineering Order Blindspots
A drawing revision changes the spec mid-job. Purchasing already ordered to the old revision. The vendor has the old drawing. The CNC is programmed to the old tolerance. Nobody connected it.
How It Works

Upload. Verify.
Monitor.

Linesentry reads your drawings, builds a requirement checklist, and watches every signal source for changes that affect active jobs.

01
Upload the Drawing PDF
Linesentry reads every note block, title block, revision triangle, and general notes section. Extracts every requirement — material specs, surface treatments, testing, marking, special processes — into a structured checklist.
02
Verify Against the Job
Every extracted requirement is cross-referenced against the work order and BOM. Anything on the drawing that isn't reflected in the job documentation is flagged before the job hits the floor.
03
Monitor All Signal Sources
Emails from vendors, Teams messages from production, engineering orders, sales updates — all ingested and cross-referenced against active jobs in real time. Status updates automatically.
04
Act on What Matters
Open Linesentry every morning and see every active job — green, yellow, or red — with the exact actions needed today. Nothing buried. Nothing missed.

Security

Proprietary drawings
stay proprietary.

Manufacturing data is sensitive by nature. Linesentry was designed from the ground up to give you control over where your data lives and who can access it.

🔒
Local AI Option
Air-gapped tier uses a local LLM running entirely on your hardware. Drawing content never leaves your facility. No external API calls required.
🛡
Row-Level Security
Every data record is scoped to the authenticated user. No user can access another user's jobs, drawings, or signals — enforced at the database level.
🔑
JWT Authentication
Every API call is verified against a signed JWT token. User identity is derived from the verified token — never from client-supplied parameters.
📋
Full Audit Trail
Every action logged — who confirmed a requirement, when a signal was actioned, what changed and when. Complete traceability for quality and compliance reviews.
