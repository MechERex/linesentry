Flagship Project
linesentry
Manufacturing operations intelligence platform. Upload engineering drawings — AI extracts every requirement. Build your BOM, generate process sequences, auto-assign requirements as gates, backwards-schedule from your due date, and scan your email for job-relevant signals. All in one place.
◈
AI Requirement Extraction
Upload engineering PDFs — Claude reads every material spec, surface treatment, testing callout, tolerance, and compliance requirement. Deduplicates on re-parse.
⬡
BOM Structure Builder
Hierarchical assembly → sub-assembly → parts tree. AI can also extract BOM structure from uploaded documents. Drag-and-drop reordering.
▦
AI Process Generation
AI generates manufacturing sequences per BOM node based on the part description, material, and requirements. Manual override always available.
⊘
Gate-Based Auto-Assignment
AI assigns each requirement to the process step that cannot proceed without it. "Which step is blocked if this requirement isn't met?"
◐
Backwards Scheduling
Set a due date — AI works backwards through every process step across every BOM node to calculate start and end dates for the entire job.
⚡
Signal Intelligence
Connect Gmail, Outlook, Teams, OneNote. AI scans for messages relevant to active jobs and auto-creates signals linked to the right job, node, and step.
◉
Status Rollup
Green / yellow / red status computed from the ground up: requirement confirmation → process step → BOM node → job. One glance tells you where you stand.
⬢
Process Map
Interactive SVG tree diagram showing parts flowing up into sub-assemblies and assemblies. Color-coded status, collapsible nodes, pan navigation.
⊞
Air-Gapped Ready
Same codebase runs fully local for aerospace and defense. Files stay on-premise, AI runs via Ollama. ITAR/EAR compliant by architecture.
